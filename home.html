SAN REMIGIO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL


San Remigio National High School is a science oriented school. It is located in Lapu-Lapu St, Poblacion, San Remigio, Cebu.
The population of the school is thousands when both students and teachers combined. When it comes to academics, San Remigio National High School (SRNHS) excels in that category. Both students and teachers also celebrate school festivals, by doing such games etc..

Provide a rich, diverse and challenging education through excellent and innovative teaching and learning. Celebrate success, promote a "Can Do" attitude and inspire each to achieve their best. Support personal development, social responsibility and a sense of self-worth.
